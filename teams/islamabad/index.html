Islamabad United "United we Win"

Islamabad United (Urdu: اسلام‌آباد یونئٹڈ) is a Pakistani professional Twenty20 franchise cricket team that competes in the Pakistan Super League. The team is based in Islamabad, the capital city of Pakistan. The franchise is owned by Leonine Global Investments through its sports entity Leonine Global Sports. The team was captained in its first season by Pakistan's most successful test captain Misbah-ul-Haq and coached by former Australian player Dean Jones. The team director is former Pakistani fast bowler Wasim Akram.
