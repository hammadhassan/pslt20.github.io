Peshawar Zalmi "Zalmi Youth of Peshawar"

Peshawar Zalmi (Urdu: پشاور زلمی‎; Pashto: پېښور زلمي‎) is a Pakistani professional franchise cricket team that represents Peshawar in Pakistan Super League.It is owned by Javed Afridi, CEO of Haier Pakistan.Shahid Afridi is the captain, Mohammad Akram is the head coach and Andy Flower is the batting consultant of the team.
