Karachi Kings "King of Kings 'Dilon k Badshah"

Karachi Kings (Urdu,:کراچی کنگز) is a Pakistani professional Twenty20 cricket team that competes in Pakistan Super League. The team is based in Karachi the provincial capital of Sindh, Pakistan.The team is currently captained by Shoaib Malik and coached by Mickey Arthur a former South African cricketer.It is owned by Salman Iqbal of the ARY Group.
